About CarePlus Hospital

CarePlus Hospital Management System is a web-based application developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It helps manage patients, doctors, appointments, and billing in an organized and user-friendly manner.


Mission

To provide efficient healthcare management through a modern and easy-to-use digital system.


Vision

To simplify hospital administration and improve patient care using technology.
